# Privacy Policy — BioScale

**Last updated:** March 19, 2026

## Introduction

BioScale is a free, open-source body composition analyzer for Bluetooth smart scales. This Privacy Policy explains how BioScale handles your data.

## Data Collection

**BioScale does NOT collect, transmit, or share any personal data.** The app is designed to work 100% offline.

### What data is stored locally on your device

- **Profile information:** Name, age, sex, height, activity level, and optional waist/hip measurements. This data is entered by you and stored only on your device.
- **Body measurements:** Weight, impedance, and calculated body composition metrics (body fat %, muscle mass, BMI, etc.). These are recorded when you use a compatible Bluetooth scale.
- **App preferences:** Language selection and weight goals.

### Where your data is stored

All data is stored **exclusively on your device** in a local SQLite database. No data is ever transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

### Data we do NOT collect

- No personal identifiers (email, phone number, account IDs)
- No location data
- No analytics or usage tracking
- No advertising identifiers
- No crash reports sent to external services
- No cookies or web tracking

## Bluetooth

BioScale uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to communicate with compatible smart scales. The Bluetooth connection is used exclusively to receive weight and impedance data from the scale. No data is sent from the app to the scale or any other device.

## Data Sharing

BioScale does **not** share your data with anyone. There are no third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or cloud services integrated into the app.

## Data Export

You may voluntarily export your measurement data as a CSV file using the app's export feature. This file is saved locally on your device, and you control how and with whom you share it.

## Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

- **Delete individual measurements** from the History screen
- **Clear all history** from the Settings screen
- **Delete all data** (factory reset) from the Settings screen
- **Uninstall the app** to remove all data permanently

## Children's Privacy

BioScale does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or collect any personal identifiers.

## Health Disclaimer

BioScale provides body composition estimates based on bioelectrical impedance analysis (BIA). These measurements are **for informational purposes only** and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for health-related decisions.
